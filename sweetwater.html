This Side of Sweetwater

Award-winning playwright Tom Stanczyk (Block Nine) explores the absurd, the romantic and the enigmatic through the lives of seven couples in his collection of short works, This Side of Sweetwater. From wide-open dreamscapes through life changing celebrations to hard choices made in the uncertain vastness of a cool desert night, lovers fall for each other, argue, swear they can't go on, and then... they go on.

Tom’s warm humor and his encouraging and helpful nature was loved by many. After his sudden passing, Lightning Rod Theater produced This Side of Sweetwater in his memory. Playwright Tony Foster contributed "Part," the final piece in the show.

Starring: Annelisha Dixon, Matthew Leddy, Keith Ewell, Cheryl Huggins, Darryl Armbruster, Brice Mitchell Williams, Anastasia Narinskiy, Marisa O'Brien, Aaron Lyons, Bryan Bellomo, Lauren Van Kurin, Demetris Hartman, Amir Levi, Brendan Farrell, and Tarah Pollock

Directed by: Amy French, Marisa O'Brien, Katherine Barcsay, Paul Hoan Zeidler, Shaina Rosenthal

Produced by: Paul Hoan Zeidler, Tony Foster, and Charlie Pacello

Photography: Katherine Barcsay
